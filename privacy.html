PRIVACY

Private beta means simple data rules.

This is a pre-launch privacy summary for waitlist collection. It should be reviewed by counsel before public launch or production API processing.

What we collect

Name, email, selected use case, and any message you voluntarily send us while joining the waitlist or contacting Mixingo.

Why we collect it

To manage beta access, prioritize language coverage, invite developer testers, and send useful product updates.

What we do not sell

We do not sell waitlist data. We do not use submitted waitlist details for third-party advertising.

Google Sheets waitlist

The beta form can submit to Google Apps Script and store responses in a Google Sheet controlled by Mixingo.

Translation data

Production API retention rules are not final. They must be documented before general availability.

Contact

Email hello@mixingo.app for access, deletion, or privacy questions.
