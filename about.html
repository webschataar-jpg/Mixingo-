WHY MIXINGO

Language was never messy.
Software was too rigid.

Multilingual people do not pause to choose one language before speaking. We switch, borrow, shorten, transliterate and adapt. Mixingo exists because software should meet people where they already are.

OUR FOCUS

Narrow on purpose

We are not starting with every document, camera or localization workflow. We are starting with plain text and trying to do one difficult job exceptionally well.

Human over literal

A technically correct sentence can still sound completely wrong. We care about the meaning, relationship and tone behind the words.

Built in public

Mixingo is pre-launch. Early users will help define which hybrid languages, product flows and developer tools matter first.
